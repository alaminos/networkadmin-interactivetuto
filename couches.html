OSI model

We can think of the human body as having different parts, for example: skin, flesh and bones, or maybe head, trunk and legs, two different models to represent the body. But it is just a representation, that is a conceptual model. Same goes with the OSI model, it is a representation of the network, it is a tool or framework that we can use to communicate about the network.

OSI means Open Systems Interconnection.

Layers

The components of the osi model are called layers. They do follow an order of execution. Data, in various formats, goes through them, passing from one to the next.
